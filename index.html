Introduction

Hundreds of years ago, demons opened portals between our world and Hell. They came through and pillaged our towns, wrecking havoc in our peacefull kingdom.
But after the first era of pannic, the Church of Osesis pressed the king to raise an army to fight against those horrible creatures. Every able man was enlisted, and soon the demons were pushed back to their realm. But this was not the end.
The Church recruited the best fighters it could find, to create an army of elite soldiers wich goal was to put an end to the demonic menace once and for all.
They were called the crusaders, and their mission, was to fight demons down in Hell.
