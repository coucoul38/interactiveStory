Interactive story : The Gates of Hell

>Use the stone on the key

You choose to use the blue gem to fuel the key.
You put it in the emplacement of the same shape, and it starts to cast a powerfull blue light. You lift the key and a transparent door lock appears in front of you.
You put the key in, and turn it.
This opens more portals between Hell and the Overworld at strategic locations, greatly facilitating the logistics of the human army.
The crusaders rapidly strike everywhere in Hell, and the war is soon finished thanks to you.
