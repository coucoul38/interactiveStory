Interactive story : The Gates of Hell

>Use the stone on the mirror

You pick up the mirror and put the blue stone in its handle.
Following this, the stone starts to glow very bright and your reflexion in the mirror gives place to visions of humans massacring demons.
Outraged by what this malefic item tries to make you believe, you throw it at the ground, breaking it. This frees a powerful demon trapped inside, which freezes you to death in its rage.
You broke the artifact by fear of it being malefic, but it was only showing you the truth.

This is what you get for blindly following orders without questionning yourself.
