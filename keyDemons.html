Interactive story : Title

>Put it in the Key of Doom

You choose to use your red gem to fuel the Key of Doom.
You put it in the emplacement of the same shape, and it starts to cast a powerfull red light.
You lift the key and a transparent door lock appears in front of you.
You put the key in, and turn it.
This closes all portals between Hell and the Overworld, but you immediatly feel a pain in your chest as you run out of breath.
By closing the portals, you broke the magical link between you and your homerealm, condemning you to a painfull death.
You will be remembered as the hero who offered his life to save the demon realm from the humans.
