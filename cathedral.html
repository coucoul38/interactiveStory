>Go to the cathedral

You walk a few minutes before arriving in front of this giant building.
Openning the front gate, you notice a mysterious staircase, and curiosity pushes you to go down in the undergrounds.
This is quite a maze, but a dim lightinh helps you find the end of it.
It is a room carved in the rock, with two pedestals in the middle.
The pedestal on the left holds a small golden handheld mirror and the other a giant tin key.
These might be some of those magical artifacts you've heard of, that could change the tide of the war once and for all.
Comming closer, you notice a shining blue stone on the floor.
You pick it up, wash the blood off of it and see it is the same shape as the holes in the artifacts.
If you want to activite them, you'll need to use this stone, but you only got one.
