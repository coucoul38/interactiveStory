Part 3 : The town

You raise your hands and tell them you're not here to fight, but to help them.
You tell them that there's a human camp on the other side of the portal, and that crusaders will kills all of them if they find the town.
They seem skeptical, and they come your way and search you.
One of the guard finds the red gem you took previously, and frantically asks you were you found it.
You tell them you just found it in a chest, and they explain you it is a magical stone used to fuel artifacts.
You've heard of those before, powerful magical objects that could turn the tide of the war forever.
They tell you are the chosen one, and that only you only should decide what to do with the stone.
They then say they'll escort you to the artifacts.

You follow the guards, going deeper and deeper inside the city's walls, finally stopping in front of what seems to be a cathedral.
The doors open, and you are blown away by how big this structure actually is.
Giant arches support a ceiling so high a dragon could fit inside.
You never though demons could build such piece of architectural art.
A few steps later, a demon girl dressed as a nun come your way and ask the guards why they're bringing you here.
The guards explain everything, and she promptly agreess to show you the artifacts.
As you follow in the cathedral's underground networks, she come closer to explain you what those artifacts are.
She tells you that they are keeping two of them : the Mirror of Apocalypse and the Key of Doom, but those are so ancient that everyone forgot what their use is.
You finally enter a large cavern, with the two magical items waiting for you on stone tables.
The artifacts look as described by the nun, one beeing a handheld mirror and the other a comically large key.
The nun tells you the magical gem you posess can only be bind to one of them, and so you have to make a choice.
